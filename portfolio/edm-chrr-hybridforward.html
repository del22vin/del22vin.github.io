Microsoft Hybrid Forward Summit

You’ve been invited!

Join us over 2 days with thought leaders from Finastra, Government of Alberta, Great Place to Work, Nutrien, City of Brampton, Forrester Research and more across Canada and Microsoft as we share best practices for crafting better employee experiences in the new era of flexible work.

Day 1 topics include:

● Hybrid – A Path to Canada’s Recovery & Productivity
● Why Embracing Hybrid Work Matters featuring Forrester Research
● An Employee-First Approach to Hybrid featuring Microsoft Canada HR
● Using Data to Define the Workplace of the Future featuring Finastra


Day 2 topics include:

● Making Wellbeing Central to Your Hybrid Strategy featuring LifeWorks
● The Workplace Transformation: Designing Spaces for Inclusion & Interaction
● Using Hybrid as a Moment to Rethink & Digitize Everything featuring Nutrien
● Securing a New World of Hybrid Work featuring Government of Alberta
● The Blueprint to Creating a Great Place to Work featuring Great Place to Work
● Redefining Citizen & Employee Experiences in a Hybrid Reality featuring City of Brampton


Be sure to sign up for both Day 1 and Day 2 of our conference today!
